Manchester City's History

Commonly known as Manchester City or simply City, is a professional football club based in Manchester, England. Here's a brief history of the club:
Manchester City was founded in 1880 as St. Mark's (West Gorton) before becoming Ardwick Association Football Club in 1887. The club officially became Manchester City in 1894.
Manchester City won the English First Division title in the 1967-68 season and the FA Cup in 1969, establishing themselves as a force in English football. Manchester City's recent achievements have solidified its position among the elite football clubs, and the team continues to be a major contender in both domestic and international competitions.
